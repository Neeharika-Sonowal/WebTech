7 DAYS OF A WEEK

The 7-day week is the international standard week (ISO 8601) used by the majority of the world. Monday is the first day of the week. It is followed by Tuesday, Wednesday, Thursday, Friday, and Saturday. Sunday is the 7th and last day of the week.

Superscript formatting in mathematical writing is to show an exponent, or a number to the power of another number.For example 22 is 4.
Subscript formatting is used in scientific formula representaions. For example H2O is water.
